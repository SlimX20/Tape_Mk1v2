🎛️ Tape MK1 — Full App Store Description

Tape MK1 is a lightweight, browser-based groovebox designed for fast, intuitive beat creation. It brings the hands-on feel of classic hardware sequencers into a simple digital interface, letting you sketch rhythms, basslines, and musical ideas instantly — without setup, plugins, or distractions.

Built around a 32-step sequencer, Tape MK1 focuses on immediacy and flow. Every element is designed to keep you in the creative zone: click steps, shape grooves, adjust swing, and hear results in real time. It’s a tool for ideas, not complexity.

⸻

🥁 Make Beats Instantly

Create drum patterns with a clear 32-step grid for kick, snare, and hi-hat. Build tight grooves or experimental rhythms in seconds. The interface is designed to feel fast, responsive, and musical from the very first click.

⸻

🎸 Built-in Bass Sequencer

Add melodic movement with a dedicated bass grid. Choose notes from a simple scale-based system and shape evolving basslines that lock into your rhythm instantly. Perfect for sketching ideas or building full loops.

⸻

🎚️ Hands-On Mixing

Balance your track with an integrated mixer for kick, snare, hi-hat, and bass. Adjust levels in real time to shape the energy of your groove without leaving the creative flow.

⸻

🌊 Groove & Feel

Tape MK1 includes swing control and per-step velocity, allowing you to move beyond rigid sequencing into more human, dynamic rhythm. From tight electronic patterns to loose, off-grid grooves, the feel is in your hands.

⸻

🎲 Instant Inspiration

Stuck? Use built-in random generators for drums or bass to spark new ideas instantly. Tape MK1 is designed not just for control, but for discovery — helping you break patterns and find new directions quickly.

⸻

🧠 Minimal, Focused Design

No menus, no installation, no plugins. Tape MK1 runs directly in your browser using the Web Audio API. It’s intentionally minimal so you can focus entirely on sound and rhythm.

⸻

🚀 Key Features

* 32-step drum sequencer
* Kick, snare, and hi-hat programming
* Bass sequencer with scale-based notes
* Real-time playback with visual step indicator
* Swing control for groove shaping
* Velocity per step for dynamic expression
* Mixer for balancing all elements
* Random pattern generation
* Instant clear/reset functions
* Runs entirely in the browser

⸻

🎯 Designed For

* Beat sketching and idea generation

* Fast musical experimentation
* Hardware-style sequencing workflows

Tape MK1 is not meant to replace full DAWs. It is a creative instrument for capturing ideas quickly — a space where rhythm comes first, and complexity stays out of the way.
